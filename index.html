Explore the Beautiful Island of Siquijor

Siquijor is a small island province located in the Central Visayas region of the Philippines, south of Cebu and Bohol. It is known for its beautiful beaches, waterfalls, diving spots, and mystical folklore. Because of its peaceful atmosphere and natural beauty, the island has become a favorite destination for travelers looking for a relaxing and unique island experience.

Island Life

Local Delicacies

Don't miss out on "Pan de Monja" and the famous Siquijor Peanut Brittle, common treats found across the island's local markets.

Beachfront Hotels

From luxury resorts in San Juan to cozy eco-lodges, there are plenty of stays right on the white sand beaches.

Expert Guides

Local guides are available at every major attraction to help you navigate the falls, caves, and mountain trails safely.

How to Get to Siquijor

Here are some simple ways for travelers to reach the beautiful island of Siquijor.

From Cebu

Travelers can take a ferry from Cebu City to Siquijor. The trip usually takes around 4 to 5 hours depending on the ferry schedule.

From Dumaguete

Visitors can ride a ferry from Dumaguete Port to Siquijor. The travel time is about 1 hour and it is one of the fastest routes.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Siquijor is during the dry season from November to May when the weather is sunny and ideal for beach trips and outdoor activities.
